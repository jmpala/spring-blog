About me...

Since the first time I've got a computer, on the year 2000, I was amazed on the magic that surrounds the question, “How do computers work?”

I grew-up playing and trying to hack video-games. I remember using programs to change games behavior to my advantage, but still asking me, what were those programs doing under the hood?

Years later, with the advance of internet, my curiosity brought me to start learning programing by myself and landed jobs on a couple of big companies as a Functional Analyst, Java and IOS developer. There I gain experience on teamwork, several technologies and how project are managed.

Finally, this site is a mixture of a portfolio and blog, where I join my passion of programming with the things that interest me.
